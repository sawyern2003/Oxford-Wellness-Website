   Answer: Most patients experience minimal discomfort. The needles used are very

   Answer: Botox is a brand name. We use premium anti-wrinkle products that work

   deliver excellent results when administered by an experienced doctor.

   Answer: At The Oxford Wellness Doctor, anti-wrinkle injections start from

5. What are the side effects?
   Answer: Side effects are generally mild and temporary, including slight
   redness, swelling, or bruising at injection sites. Rare side effects include
   temporary drooping, which resolves naturally. Dr. Taganova's expertise
   minimizes risks.

6. Can I drive after treatment?
   Answer: Yes, you can drive immediately after anti-wrinkle injections. There's
   no downtime, and you can return to normal activities right away.

7. How long until I see results?
   Answer: Results typically become visible within 3-7 days, with full effects
   apparent after 14 days. We offer a free two-week follow-up to assess results.

   breastfeeding as a precautionary measure, though no specific risks have been
   proven.

   Answer: Anti-wrinkle injections are not suitable for pregnant or breastfeeding
