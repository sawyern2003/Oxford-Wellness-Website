DESIGN SYSTEM SPECIFICATIONS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

COLORS (Luxury Medical Spa Palette):

Primary Palette:
- Background: Pure white (#FFFFFF) or warm off-white (#FAFAF9, #F9F8F6)
- Primary text: Deep charcoal (#1A202C or #2D3748)
- Secondary text: Warm gray (#4A5568 or #718096)
- Accent 1: Soft sage/eucalyptus (#8FA998, #A4BCA6) - calming, natural
- Accent 2: Warm taupe/sand (#C8B8A8, #D4C5B9) - elegant, neutral
- Accent 3: Soft gold (#D4AF37 but muted - #C9AD7F) - luxury touch

Alternative Palette (if you prefer cooler tones):
- Accent 1: Soft blue-gray (#8C9CAD, #A5B8C5)
- Accent 2: Warm beige (#E5DDD5, #DCD4CC)
- Accent 3: Rose gold (#B76E79 but very muted)

AVOID:
- ❌ Bright blues (too corporate)
- ❌ Harsh blacks (too severe)
- ❌ Multiple bright colors (too busy)
- ❌ Neon or saturated colors

TYPOGRAPHY:

Heading Options (choose one):
- Cormorant Garamond (elegant serif, free)
- Playfair Display (classic serif, free)
- Bodoni Moda (sophisticated serif, free)
- Or elegant sans-serif: Futura, Avenir Next, Inter (with increased spacing)

Body Text:
- Inter (modern, clean, free)
- Lato (friendly, professional, free)
- Avenir Next (if budget allows)

Font Sizes:
- H1 (Section titles): 36-48px, letter-spacing: 0.02em
- H2 (Card titles): 24-28px, letter-spacing: 0.01em
- H3 (Subheadings): 20-24px
- Body: 16-18px, line-height: 1.7-1.8
- Small text: 14px

Font Weights:
- Headings: 300-400 (light to regular, not bold - more elegant)
- Body: 400 (regular)
- Emphasis: 500-600 (medium, not bold)

SPACING:

Use generous white space:
- Section padding: 80-120px top/bottom
- Between cards: 40-60px
- Card internal padding: 40-60px
- Paragraph spacing: 24-32px
- Line height: 1.7-1.8 (very readable, airy)

SHADOWS:

Subtle, elegant shadows (not harsh):
- Card shadow: 0 4px 20px rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.08)
- Hover shadow: 0 8px 30px rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.12)
- NO black shadows, use colored shadows for warmth:
  0 4px 20px rgba(200, 184, 168, 0.15) - warmer

BORDERS:

- Avoid borders on cards - use shadows instead
- If borders needed: 1px solid #E5E7EB (very subtle)
- Rounded corners: 12-16px (modern but not too round)

BUTTONS:

Primary Button Style:
- Background: Soft sage or taupe accent color
- Text: White or deep charcoal (high contrast)
- Padding: 16px 32px
- Border radius: 8px
- Font size: 16px
- Font weight: 500
- Letter spacing: 0.02em
- Hover: Slightly darker + subtle lift

Button Text:
- "Learn More" (not "Read More")
- "Explore" (elegant)
- "Discover" (luxurious)
- "View Details" (professional)
- "Book Consultation" (clear CTA)

AVOID button text:
- ❌ "Click Here"
- ❌ "Find Out More"
- ❌ "See Treatments"

ICONS:

If using icons:
- Line icons, not filled (more elegant)
- Minimal, geometric style
- Consistent stroke weight (1.5-2px)
- Subtle color (not bright)
- Or remove icons entirely and use typography/imagery

Icon Libraries to Consider:
- Feather Icons (minimal, elegant)
- Heroicons (clean, modern)
- Phosphor Icons (versatile)

HOMEPAGE PRICING STRATEGY
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

REMOVE "from £XXX" from all homepage treatment cards

WHY?
- Feels transactional, not luxurious
- Can deter high-end clients before they learn value
- Makes comparison shopping too easy
- Cheapens the premium positioning
- Medical care shouldn't lead with price

INSTEAD:
- Have a dedicated, beautiful pricing page (/pricing)
- Include "Investment" or "Fees" on individual treatment pages
- Homepage focuses on value, expertise, results
- Let patients fall in love with YOU first, then discuss price

PRICING PAGE DESIGN:
- Elegant table or list format
- Soft background
- "Investment in Yourself" or "Treatment Fees" as heading
- Include: "All treatments include comprehensive consultation and
  follow-up care"
- Package options highlighted
- "Book Consultation" CTA at bottom
